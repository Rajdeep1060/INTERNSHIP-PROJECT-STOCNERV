About TradesOnTheHouse


TradesOnTheHouse is a Joint Venture of JINEE Inc (Pennsylvania, U.S.A.) and Stocnerv Learning Pvt Limited (Jaipur, India). We aim to provide financial education with the goal of reaching financial independence. To make trading | advisory | analysis free and accessible to all, traders across the world can hop on to our platform and access the research data countrywise.

Traders can best use the research generated by our algorithm with high accuracy and integrate it with their trades. Analysis section will give them an understanding of the fundamental | technical | chart analysis with pattern targets.

With this mission & vision, we want every trader & investor across the world to make informed decisions instead of doing herd-trading.
